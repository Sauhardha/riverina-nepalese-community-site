About us

We are a Nepalese community in Wagga Wagga who share joy & support.

Our organisation was established to preserve our culture with Nepali residents living in Wagga Wagga and across the Riverina. Together we create a home that feels familiar while celebrating the region we now belong to.

We aim to pass our traditions to younger generations, offer practical support to new arrivals, and champion events that keep our community connected.

Tradition

Workshops, cultural lessons, and shared stories keep Nepalese heritage vivid for every generation.

Support

We look out for each other and coordinate help during personal or community emergencies.

Celebration

Dashain, Tihar, Teej, and more annual festivals keep the Nepali spirit alive in Riverina.

Connection

Mentoring and shared experience make it easier for new residents to settle into the region.
